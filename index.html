WHAT IS FORMULA 1 ? :
Formula 1 (F1) is the highest level of international car racing. It features fast, high-performance single-seater cars competing on tracks around the world. F1 is known for its speed, technology, and global fanbase. Each F1 season includes a series of races called Grands Prix, held in different countries. Teams like Ferrari, Mercedes, and Red Bull build their own cars and hire skilled drivers to compete, such as Lewis Hamilton and Max Verstappen. The main goal is to win races and score points. At the end of the season, the driver and team with the most points become the World Champions.
